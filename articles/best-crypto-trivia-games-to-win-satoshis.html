Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly are "satoshis"?

Satoshis, often called "sats," are the smallest unit of Bitcoin. Just like a dollar is divided into 100 cents, one Bitcoin is divided into 100 million satoshis. Earning satoshis means you're earning tiny fractions of Bitcoin, which can add up over time.

Are these crypto trivia games truly free to play?

Many of the best crypto trivia games to win satoshis are indeed free to play. They often rely on advertising revenue or optional in-app purchases for power-ups to sustain themselves. Some competitive games might require a small crypto entry fee, but there are plenty of free options available.

How do I withdraw the satoshis I win?

Typically, you'll accumulate satoshis within the game's platform. Once you reach a minimum withdrawal threshold (which varies by game), you can initiate a transfer to a personal Bitcoin wallet address that you control. It's crucial to have your own secure wallet set up before you start playing.

Is it really worth my time to play these games for satoshis?

That depends on your goals. If you're looking to get rich quick, probably not. However, if you see it as a way to learn about crypto, stay informed, and accumulate small amounts of Bitcoin over time without direct investment, then it can be very worthwhile. It’s a low-risk way to engage with the crypto economy.
